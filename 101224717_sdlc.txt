Description:
SDLC stands for Software Development Life Cycle is a process of planning, creating, testing and deploying information systems across hardware and software. 

There are 7 stages of SDLC: 
  * Stage 1: Project planing
  * Stage 2: Gathering requirements and Analysis
  * Stage 3: Design
  * Stage 4: Coding and implementation 
  * Stage 5: Testing
  * Stage 6: Deployment
  * Stage 7: Maintenance
