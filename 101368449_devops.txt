
This course aims to teach students the methods, guidelines, and optimal approaches that a DevOps professional implements in a work environment to enhance efficiency and effectiveness. Examine and assess different developer operations implementations that are accessible, and apply practical experience using widely recognized toolsets in the industry. Tackle challenging business issues by following the principles of DevOps. With DevOps, you can find innovative solutions to complex problems that help your organization work smarter, collaborate better, and deliver results more efficiently.
