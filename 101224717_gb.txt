George Brown College is also known as short name is GBC. This college is 
suitable studying for every student even international student. Because it 
is the first college to start distance education system and one of the top 
universities and colleges in Canada. There are 3 campuses, namely Casa 
Loma Campus, St.James Campus and Waterfront Campus. Specially, the Casa 
Loma Campus is the main one and is know as clean and green space of the 
college. It is located at 160 Kendal Ave, Toronto, ON Canada M5R 1M3. All the three campus are located at a place where students are nearby to restaurant, shops and stores, and in reach of a job market, as the companies play an important role in the academic life of students. After student graduates, GBC offer students someplace to find their career.
